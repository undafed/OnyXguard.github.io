Who Are We?

OnyXguard is dedicated to providing top-tier security services, ensuring the safety and protection of our clients. Our team of experts uses advanced technology and best practices to safeguard your home, office, and personal well-being.

Our services

We offer various security services, including home security, office protection, and personal bodyguard services. Our team is equipped to handle all your security needs with professionalism and care.

Home Security

We offer comprehensive home security solutions to protect your family and property. Our advanced systems include surveillance cameras, alarm monitoring, and access control, all designed to ensure the highest level of safety. With our expert team and cutting-edge technology, we provide peace of mind by securing your home against potential threats.

Office Security

Our office security services are designed to protect your business premises, employees, and assets. We offer a range of solutions, including surveillance systems, access control, and on-site security personnel, all tailored to meet your organization's specific needs. We aim to create a safe and secure work environment, allowing you to focus on your business operations without worry.

Bodyguard

Our professional bodyguard services ensure your safety and peace of mind at home, work, or during travel. Our highly trained and discreet security personnel are skilled in threat assessment, crisis management, and providing close protection. We tailor our services to your needs, offering round-the-clock protection to safeguard you and your loved ones from potential threats.
